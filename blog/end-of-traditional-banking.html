Transmission 002

The End of Traditional Banking is Here

Banks used to be vaults. Then they became databases. Tomorrow, they will just be compliance layers for programmable money.

The innovation isn't happening in the marble-floored lobbies of legacy institutions. It's happening in smart contracts, decentralized finance protocols, and fintech platforms that view capital as fluid data.

We are moving from a permission-based financial system to an access-based one. The gatekeepers are losing their grip, and the friction is being priced out of the market.

For the consumer and the builder, this means unprecedented speed. For the legacy bank? It means evolve into an API, or die.
