Transmission 003

The Ownership Mindset

The industrial revolution programmed us to be employees. Trade time for money, get a gold watch, die quietly. That model is broken.

In the digital economy, renting your time is a slow death by inflation and automation. Wealth is no longer created by hoarding wages; it is created by holding equity.

You must own something. Own equity in a startup. Own digital real estate. Own an audience. Own your intellectual property. The moment you shift your focus from "how much can I earn per hour" to "how much equity can I accumulate," the entire game changes.

Ownership is the ultimate flex. It is the only true form of security.
