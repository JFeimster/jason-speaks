Transmission 005

Activation > Motivation

I despise the word motivation. Motivation is a feeling. It is a biological spike in dopamine that fades the moment you hit a real obstacle.

You don't need to feel good to execute. You don't need a perfectly optimized morning routine. You need activation. You need to bridge the gap between intention and action in the shortest amount of time possible.

Stop waiting for the muse. Stop waiting for the perfect market conditions. Stop waiting until you "feel" ready. Force the function. Activate.
