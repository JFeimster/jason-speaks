Transmission 001

AI Won't Replace You (But The Operator Using It Will)

The anxiety around artificial intelligence is entirely misplaced. People are staring at ChatGPT and Midjourney, wondering if their jobs will exist in 36 months.

Let me give you the brutal truth: The algorithm doesn't want your job. It doesn't want anything. It is a tool.

What you should be afraid of is the 23-year-old kid in his bedroom who just figured out how to chain three APIs together to do your 40-hour work week in 12 minutes.

The future belongs to the editors, the curators, and the directors. If you view yourself as a pure laborer in the knowledge economy, you are in danger. If you view yourself as an operator managing a fleet of digital interns, you are about to see unprecedented leverage.

Stop playing defense. Learn the prompts. Build the workflows. Be the operator.
