# Brand Notes

## Visual Style

Neobrutalist × Futurist Fintech × Cyberpunk Hustler

## Brand Feel

Inspirational, bold, high-energy, future-facing, street-smart, polished, and entrepreneurial.

## Color Direction

- Black / near-black
- Off-white
- Electric lime
- Hot magenta
- Cyber blue
- Acid yellow

## Design Motifs

- Thick borders
- Hard shadows
- Glitch details
- Dashboard UI cards
- Sticker-style labels
- Oversized typography
