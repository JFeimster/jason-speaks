The Manifesto

1. Nobody is coming to save you. The institutions are slow. The algorithms are fast. Your leverage is entirely your responsibility.


2. Friction is a feature, not a bug. Where there is discomfort, there is opportunity. Monetize the hard things.


3. Equity over salary. Renting out your time is how you survive. Owning assets is how you build an empire.


4. Execute before you are ready. Perfect timing is a myth created by cowards to justify their inaction. Launch the beta. Fix it live.
